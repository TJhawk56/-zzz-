Agents are playable characters that you can recruit to fight in your team. Each Agent has a role, like attacking, defending, or stunning, and they belong to a faction. action role-playing game. The player assumes the role of a Proxy (the protagonist known as Wise or Belle), a character who helps others explore alternate dimensions called Hollows.

W-Engines are essential equipment that grant stat boosts and provide benefits from a main effect, so you'll want to enhance their level and star rating, equip them to characters, and potentially unlock cosmetic effects.

Bangboos are intelligent, knee-sized devices initially designed to assist New Eridu residents during Hollow disasters, but now serve as versatile helpers, including providing combat support and assistance within Hollows.
